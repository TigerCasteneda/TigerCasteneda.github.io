Our Product

Using adversarial example technology in reverse, we embed imperceptible perturbations that disrupt AI feature extraction, keeping your images safe and your visual experience intact. Unlike traditional defenses(watermark, blurring) which are easy to remove or passive AI detection engines, our product protects images proactively, making data inherently unreadable to malicious AI.

Human-Imperceptible

Adversarial perturbations are strategically embedded in target images, ensuring zero loss in visual quality for human observers.

AI-Ineffective

Embedded interference disrupts the feature extraction consistency of machine learning models，hence making it unreliable and unstable for AI models to process accurately.

Multi-Modal Protection

Simultaneously safeguards faces, digits, and text on images containing complex features like IDs, passports, licenses, etc.

About Our Mission

We are a research-driven team founded on the principle of using AI's own strengths to create a more secure digital ecosystem.

Research indicates that approximately 22% of AI-generated faces are now convincing enough to fool human observers, and surveys show that 75% of the public is concerned about AI-driven scams. In a landscape where AI-generated fraud is growing exponentially, traditional defensive measures are reactive and insufficient. Our team of specialists in adversarial machine learning and computer vision has pioneered a proactive solution—turning a vulnerability into the ultimate shield.

Vision & Future

Our roadmap extends beyond static images. We are actively developing protection for video streams and audio data, and exploring applications in creative media, healthcare, and autonomous systems to set new standards for data provenance.
